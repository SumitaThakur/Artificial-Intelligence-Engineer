Conclusions

  1. Autopilot-related fatalities have increased year-over-year.
  2. Majority of events occurred in the United States.
  3. Most fatal collisions involved the Tesla Model S and Model 3.
  4. Almost all accidents involve a single fatality, though some involve 24.
  5. Tesla-driver deaths remain a significant portion of total fatalities.
  6. Events involving cyclists and pedestrians remain relatively low but present.
  7. Verified autopilot deaths are a smaller subset of total reported deaths, suggesting possible under-reporting or unverified cases.
